How To Play
Goal
Get as close as possible to the target number in five sums or less.
Rules
Each number can be used once, including sum results.
Numbers will change to green when used.
Results
Complete three daily rounds and score as close to 100% as possible.
Your score is based on:
1
how close you get to the target number
2
how quickly you submit your answer
Exact answers score highest.
Answers more than 10 away, skipped rounds, or timeouts score 0%.
Your final daily score is the average of all three rounds.
